Artwork

Themes in Brand Imagery
Photography

Guidelines:

  • Photography must always be gradient mapped using the Brand Neutrals
  • Gradient map effect makes the photography easy to place type or graphics over

Themes:

  • Architecture - Minimalistic, organic forms are ideal. Focus on lines that extend beyond the frame as well as repeating patterns
  • Water - Avoid high noise imagery (such as splashes). Best to use images that capture the fluidity of water
  • San Francisco - Images of our HQ city's landmarks are acceptable


Graphics + Photography

Guidelines:

  • Photography should be modified to follow the Brand Colors
  • Unusual rotations and framing are ideal, avoid literal representations
  • Strong Compositions - Lines extend beyond the frame, diagonal lines preferred, create sense of dynamic motion

Themes:

  • Nature vs. Technology - Combine images in a harmonious way, the two should flow together seamlessly
  • Wrangling Data - Use graphics and photography to communicate containment of data. In above example: net + clouds
  • Epic Scale - Combinations that communicate scale are ideal, this can be achieved through dense patterns and vanishing points


Graphics

Guidelines:

  • No literal representations
  • Linework + gradients

Themes:

  • Meshes - Also known as 3D wireframes, usually abstract and resembling waves or mountains. Position at interesting angles over gradient backgrounds
  • Gradients - Subtle gradients everywhere, helps the graphics feel more rich. Avoid solid backgrounds unless dark grey or offwhite
  • Patterns & Repetition - Graphics depicting dense and repetitive patterns are ideal
